Why VR for Pharma Training?

Safe, repeatable practice

Let students make mistakes in a virtual cleanroom instead of your real lab. Reset, replay, and refine technique as many times as needed.

Standardised assessments

Every student faces the same validated scenario, removing subjectivity and capturing objective performance data.

Faster certification

College faculty track completion, request certificates, and let AScienti Sols generate digital credentials instantly.

How AScienti Sols Works

Step 1
Institution Onboarding

Pharmacy colleges partner with AScienti Sols and select VR modules (e.g., aseptic processing, dispensing, clinical scenarios).

Step 2
Student Access

Students receive login credentials after fee payment and college approval. They use VR headsets / compatible devices to attempt scenarios.

Step 3
Performance Tracking

Completion, adherence to SOPs, and mistakes are recorded and visible to faculty on the web dashboard.

Step 4
Certification

Once criteria are met, admins trigger certificate generation and students download their verified pharma training certificates.
